# Reviewer Questions Template

**Purpose:** Get targeted, actionable feedback from reviewers instead of generic "please review" requests.

**Use with draft PRD:** Send these questions along with the draft PRD to get specific, helpful feedback.

---

## Structured Review Questions

**[Reviewer Name], I've attached the draft PRD for [feature name]. Rather than asking for general review, I have specific questions:**

### The 5 Standard Questions

**1. Problem Statement Accuracy**
Is the problem statement accurate to what you see operationally?

**2. Scope Completeness**  
Are there workflows, payment types, or partners missing from scope?

**3. Solution Approach**
Are the functional requirements solving the right problem, or should the approach change?

**4. Team Assignments**
Are the team assignments and personas correct?

**5. Missing Knowledge**
What would you add, remove, or change in the requirements?

---

## Format for Responses

**You can respond however is easiest:**
- Slack reply with answers
- Comments directly in the doc  
- Quick call to walk through it
- Email with bullet points

**The goal:** Help me get this right before final drafting, not catch errors after it's "done."

---

## Additional Context-Specific Questions (Add as needed)

**Operational Questions:**
- How does this workflow actually work today in your team?
- What's the volume/frequency we're dealing with?
- What specific manual steps would this eliminate?

**Technical Questions:**
- Does this align with the current system architecture?
- Are there existing APIs/services we should leverage?
- What are the performance implications?

**Business Questions:**
- Is this the right priority given other initiatives?
- How does this connect to customer commitments or partner agreements?
- What's the business impact if we delay this 6 months?
