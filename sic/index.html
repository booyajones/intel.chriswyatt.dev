Vector 1: The Lorenz Curve of Spend Inequality

The So What?

The AP supplier base is not distributed evenly; it exhibits a severe Pareto power-law dynamic. Our calculated Gini Coefficient reveals the exact scale of spend concentration at the very top.

Strategic Implication: The top 5% of suppliers control over 80% of the working capital leverage. These are mandatory dynamic discounting targets. Conversely, the remaining bottom 95% of suppliers—the extreme long-tail—generate immense operational friction (check printing costs, manual reconciliation) but represent less than 20% of the dollar volume.

Action: Rapid ACH batch conversion and broad-brush Virtual Card enrollment for the long tail; bespoke Supply Chain Finance for the top 5%.
